About the Website

This website will allow you to search for electric vehicles and plug-in hybrid vehicles that will meet your needs as a consumer. Whether you are looking to buy a new car or just learn about the vehicles on the market, you will be able to find what you are looking for here.

You will be able to access data about the newest models viable in the Canadian market. You can search for these vehicles either by brand or by the exact model, and you will be able to compare different vehicles to see which one is more suitable for you. Also, If you make an account, after logging in, you will be able to save vehicles to your wishlist, so that you can view them later.

It's time to switch from purely gasoline-powered vehicles to environmentally friendly alternatives. We have collected all the facts, and now you can find the best electric vehicle for you!

Meet the Team

This website was developed for an undergraduate term project for the MIE350 course at the University of Toronto. The developing team consists of 7 undergraduate industrial engineering students.
