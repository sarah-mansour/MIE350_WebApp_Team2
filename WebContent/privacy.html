What Information We Collect

This website can be used for searching and comparing cars without you providing any personal information for us to save. If you wish to save a wishlist, you will need to sign up for an account. To do so, you must provide an email and password for this website for us to uniquely identify you and to send you a password reset link if you forget your password. Providing your first name and last name are optional for us to communicate with you by name.

Editing and Deleting Your Information

All of your information can be viewed and edited from your account page. Your first name and/or last name can be deleted from our website and database from your account page. If you would like to delete your email, this will terminate your account and delete all saved wishlists, this can also be done at the bottom of your account page.
